Product Size Guide

  • Product sizes are the measurements of the actual product.
  • Fit may vary, even for product/garments which are the same size.
  • The sizes indicated for products which feature tucks and pleats are measured with the tucks and pleats opened up.
  • Depending the the charateristics of the fabric used, there could be a sizing difference up to 1cm.
